Reality Check

Life is not an ashtray,
The burns leave a mark.
Must race the sun to cheat the day,
and face the fear to beat the dark.

Life is not a zero-sum game,
The prisoner who confesses, is set free.
We are all one and the same,
A bit selflessness-in-selfishness is all we need.

Life is not an instruction,
The cynic didn't know what his aim was.
What's left after destruction,
can't be destroyed further by the same cause.

Life is an acid trip.
Enjoy it till the last drip.
